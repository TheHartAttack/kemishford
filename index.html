Kemishford Painters & Decorators are a company that offers a friendly and professional service, dealing with all aspects of interior and exterior painting and decorating. The emphasis of our service is in paying attention to detail, and we place a great importance on understanding and interpreting our client's requirements with communication being the absolute key to this objective. This follows on through to the work we do and how we do it. Flooring, furniture, bathroom suites, kitchen units and anything else not being painted is covered over with protective polythene and/or dust sheets. Preparation is the key to a successful job - surfaces are always properly prepared or rubbed down to ensure a good key for the new paint and a smooth finish. We will always follow the paint manufacturers recommended application systems to ensure the best possible finish.

We are more than happy to advise clients on appropriate and suitable products to meet the required application. This is by no means the limit of our remit, an almost unspoken rule, but probably the most important element of our business is that we are respectful, polite and sympathetic to the daily routine of our clients. We are acutely aware that we are trusted guests, and will maintain a clean and tidy environment whenever possible.

Our services include...

  • Interior and exterior painting and decorating
  • Re-decorating existing properties and decorating new builds/extensions
  • Airless spray painting
  • Wallpapering
  • Wood and laminate floor installation
  • Skirting and coving
  • Curtain rail and blind fitting
